French Fries

Ingredients Directions
5 pounds russet potatoes Peel and rinse the potatoes. Then cut them into sticks by cutting the potato in 4 or 5 vertical pieces, and then cutting each piece into sticks.
Vegetable or peanut oil, for frying Place them in a large bowl and cover with cold water. Allow them to soak, 2 to 3 hours. (You can also stick them in the fridge and let them soak overnight.)
Sea salt When you're ready to make the fries, drain off the water and lay the potatoes on 2 baking sheets lined with paper towels. Blot with paper towels to dry them.Heat a few inches of oil in a heavy pot to 300 degrees F. In 3 or 4 batches, cook the potatoes until soft, 4 to 5 minutes per batch. They should not be brown at this point! You just want to start the cooking process. Remove each batch and drain on new/dry paper towels. >Once all the potatoes have been fried at 300 degrees F, turn up the heat until the oil reaches 400 degrees F. When the oil's hot, start frying the potatoes in batches again, cooking until the fries are golden and crisp. Remove the potatoes from the oil and drain on paper towels.Sprinkle with sea salt and dive in!

Wisconsin Mac n Cheese

IngredientsDirections
1 1/3 cups small elbow macaroni Cook macaroni in medium saucepan of boiling salted water until tender but still firm to bite. Drain.
1 slice whole wheat bread Meanwhile, grind bread in processor to fine crumbs; transfer to small bowl. Melt butter in medium saucepan. Mix 1/2 tablespoon into crumbs. Add flour to remaining butter; whisk over medium heat 2 minutes. Gradually whisk in milk and broth. Bring to boil, whisking constantly. Add green onions. Whisk 2 minutes longer. Remove from heat. Add cheese; stir until melted.
2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) butter, 2 tablespoons all purpose flour, 3/4 cup low-fat milk, 3/4 cup canned vegetable broth, 2 green onions, thinly sliced and 1 1/2 cups (packed) grated sharp cheddar cheese Preheat broiler. Mix macaroni into sauce. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Spoon into 9-inch pie plate. Sprinkle crumbs over. Broil until crumbs brown, about 2 minutes, and serve.

Apple Pie

Ingredients Directions
7 tart apples, peeled and cored Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Cut the apples into 1/4 inch slices.
1 cup white sugar, 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon and 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour In a mixing bowl, combine sugar, cinnamon, flour, nutmeg (or apple pie spice), and salt; mix thoroughly. Pour the spice mix over the apples and stir until the apples are coated.
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg and salt Line one crust in a 9 inch deep dish pie pan. Place the apples in the pie crust. Dot the apple filling evenly with butter. Fit the top crust over the apples. Press the crust down gently and cut 3 or 4 slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape while the pie bakes
2 recipes unbaked pie shells and 1 tablespoon butter Bake in the preheated oven 50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown. Check the pie after the first 30 minutes of cooking: if the crust is already browned reduce the heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) to allow the apples to cook without the crust burning.
